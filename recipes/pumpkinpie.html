Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Simple Pumpkin Pie

Learn how to make pumpkin pie with just four ingredients, an unbaked pie crust, and this easy pumpkin pie recipe. Show off your baking talent by serving this "easy as pie" classic for Thanksgiving or anytime you need a delicious dessert.

Ingerdients

  • 9 inch unbaked pie crust
  • 16 ounce can pumpkin puree
  • 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin spice

Directions

  • Step 1

    • Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Fit pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and place it on a baking sheet.

  • Step 2

    • Combine pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, and pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl: mix untill combined. Pour into the pie crust

  • Step 3

    • Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue to bake untill filling is set, 35 to 40 minutes.

  • Step 4

    • Remove the oven, set on wire rack, and cool completely before serving.
